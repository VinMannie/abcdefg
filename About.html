about the Game

In this custom Super Mario Bros game, Mario or Luigi must avoid getting ambushed by the enemies before time runs out.
In order to finish the game, you must beat all levels provided until the pink castle (which is considered the final level).
Level and map placement is random, like Super Mario Bros 3.

You are given powerups throughout the game:

Mushroom: This allows you to run a little faster than usual.

Fringe Leaf Mario/Luigi: You gain the ability to shoot fireballs and fly like the Mandolorian.

If you get hit by an enemy, you will lose these powerups as well as some coins you earn.
You get a 1-up if you get 50 coins, or you turn a koopa shell into food
(which also powers you up as Fringe Leaf Mario/Luigi immediately).

To exit the game at any time, or switch characters, click the Custom Mario Bros logo up on the top (that is underlined).
